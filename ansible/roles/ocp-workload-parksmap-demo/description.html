Parksmap

Parksmap demo showing the app used in the RoadShow FY 17 labs. It's a microservices application using SpringBoot and JBoss EAP. ​This demo walks the audience through​ ​the process​ and concepts of developing an application on OpenShift.

Products and Projects:

  • OpenShift Application Runtimes
