Persistant storage management in OpenShift with the Portworx Operator

In this Red Hat OpenSHift Container Platform (OCP) workshop, you will use the Portwrox Operator to manage persistent storage in an OpenShift 4 cluster. This lab is 30 minutes, hands-on, and comes with the Portworx Operator installed, as well as EBS volumes .
